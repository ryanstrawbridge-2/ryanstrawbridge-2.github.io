## Problem

Water constantly leaked from the top of the faucet, running down the back and soaking the counter.

## What I did

Took inspiration from wine bottle drip rings to catch the runoff. Measured the faucet neck diameter with a caliper and added a touch of clearance for a snug, leak-tight grip. Designed the channel geometry to redirect water away from the counter, and FDM printed the part on our apartment printer.
